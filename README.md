Website Overview: Voice of the Voiceless
Voice of the Voiceless is an online platform established in 2026 to support and empower sexual assault survivors in South Africa. It serves as a "digital lighthouse," offering a safe space to share stories, access resources, and advocate for social change. 
1. Core Mission & Vision
•	Mission: To provide a secure, compassionate platform 

that amplifies survivor voices, facilitates justice, and advocates for a South Africa free from sexual violence.
•	Vision: To dismantle the culture of silence and become the primary resource for survivor empowerment.
•	Philosophy: Turning victimhood into agency through community and information.
2. Key Objectives

•	Breaking the Silence: Raising national awareness about South Africa’s status as an epicenter for sexual violence.
•	Resource Accessibility: Directly connecting users to Thuthuzela Care Centres, legal aid, and professional counseling.
•	Safe Advocacy: Engaging users with social justice initiatives and movements like Women for Change.
3. Proposed Features & Functionality

•	The "Wall of Silence": A moderated, anonymous space for survivors to share testimonials and find solidarity.
•	Educational Hub: Fact sheets regarding survivor rights and national crime statistics.
•	Action Portal: A centralized area for petitions, donation links for partner NGOs, and information on marches.
•	Safety First: Inclusion of a "Quick Exit" feature to ensure 

user safety if they are interrupted while browsing.
4. Design & User Experience (UX)
•	Trauma-Informed Aesthetics: The site will use a minimalist interface with a calming color palette, including Alice Blue (F2F7FF), Periwinkle (B2C9FF), and Wisteria (B49EF4).
•	Language: Usage of clear, non-triggering language throughout the site.

•	Mobile Optimization: Fully responsive design for smartphones to ensure accessibility for all survivors.
•	Security: Prioritizing user anonymity through rigorous security
