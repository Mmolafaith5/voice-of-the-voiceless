A Digital Lighthouse for Sexual Assault Survivors in South Africa

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide a secure, compassionate platform that amplifies survivor voices, facilitates justice, and advocates for a South Africa free from sexual violence.

Vision

To dismantle the culture of silence and become the primary resource for survivor empowerment.

Philosophy: Turning victimhood into agency through community and information.

Key Objectives

  • Breaking the Silence: Raising national awareness about South Africa’s status as an epicenter for sexual violence.
  • Resource Accessibility: Directly connecting users to Thuthuzela Care Centres, legal aid, and professional counseling.
  • Safe Advocacy: Engaging users with social justice initiatives and movements like Women for Change.
